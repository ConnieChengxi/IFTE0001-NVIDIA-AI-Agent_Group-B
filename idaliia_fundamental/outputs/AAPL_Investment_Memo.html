2. Company Overview

Apple Inc (AAPL) is a USA-based company in the CONSUMER ELECTRONICS industry within the TECHNOLOGY sector, listed on NASDAQ with a market capitalization of approximately $3.8T.

Apple maintains a competitive position in the market through its strong brand recognition, innovative product design, and integrated ecosystem of hardware, software, and services.
