2. Company Overview

NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA) is a USA-based company in the SEMICONDUCTORS industry within the TECHNOLOGY sector, listed on NASDAQ with a market capitalization of approximately $4.7T.

NVIDIA Corporation operates as a semiconductor company, generating revenue primarily through the design and sale of graphics processing units (GPUs) and system on a chip units (SoCs) for the gaming and professional markets. Key products include GeForce for gamers, Quadro for professionals, and Tegra for mobile and automotive applications, all of which have solidified NVIDIA's position as a leader in the GPU market. NVIDIA's competitive advantage lies in its robust intellectual property portfolio, high-performance GPUs, and deep learning technology, which have positioned it at the forefront of the AI and machine learning revolution. Headquartered in Santa Clara, California, NVIDIA has a global footprint with significant market presence in North America, Europe, and Asia.
